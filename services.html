Our Services & Troubleshooting Resources

We help you build, optimize, and fix your PC. Below are the best trusted resources when something goes wrong.

Troubleshooting Guides & Tools

  • Tom's Hardware — Excellent troubleshooting guides, component reviews, and forums.
  • r/buildapc — Best community for PC building and troubleshooting help.
  • r/techsupport — Great for general PC problems and hardware issues.
  • PCPartPicker — Check compatibility and find the best prices.
  • UserBenchmark — Quick way to test if your hardware is performing as expected.
  • HWInfo — Best free tool to monitor temperatures, voltages, and hardware health.
  • MemTest86 — Best tool to test if your RAM is faulty.
  • CrystalDiskInfo — Check the health of your SSD/HDD.
  • Linus Tech Tips — Great troubleshooting videos and build guides.
  • Gamers Nexus — In-depth hardware reviews and failure analysis.
  • iFixit — Best for repair guides and teardown videos.
